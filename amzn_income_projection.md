## Tax Considerations (Australia)

RSU vesting is taxed as **ordinary income** at your marginal rate:

| Income Bracket (AUD) | Tax Rate |
|----------------------|----------|
| $0 – $18,200 | 0% |
| $18,201 – $45,000 | 16% |
| $45,001 – $135,000 | 30% |
| $135,001 – $190,000 | 37% |
| $190,001 – | 45% |
